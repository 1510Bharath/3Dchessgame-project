    How to play

    Move a piece: tap it, then tap a highlighted square. Legal destinations glow gold; the king glows red when in check.

    AI opponents: toggle “AI” on a side and choose a level from 1 (relaxed) to 10 (ruthless). Two AI sides can play each other.

    Online play: one player taps “Create room” and reads out the 6-character code; the other taps “Join”, types the code, and the board connects directly between the two devices.

    Camera: drag to orbit, pinch or scroll to zoom.

    About Oakwood Chess

    A self-contained 3D chess set: rendered with WebGL, refereed by a full chess rules engine, and opposed by a minimax search engine running in a background thread.

    Online games connect two browsers directly over a peer-to-peer link — there is no game server, so moves travel straight between the two devices.
